Senior Backend Developer - Python, AWS, Kubernetes

Become part of our Krones.digital community and join us as a Senior Backend Developer – Python, AWS, Kubernetes!

We’re looking for an experienced developer who will play a key role in building high-quality, scalable applications. You’ll be responsible for designing and implementing robust, testable code and ensuring our solutions meet the highest standards.

Your mission at Krones.digital

  • Develop and maintain scalable backend solutions using modern technologies and frameworks.
  • Drive the entire lifecycle of our solutions – from design and implementation to integration and operation.
  • Implement interfaces and microservices to process data from diverse sources and enable seamless information exchange.
  • Ensure the scalability and performance of our backend systems to support a growing user base.
  • Guarantee smooth integration and communication with platform and cloud components.
  • Apply security best practices to protect data integrity and user privacy.
  • Collaborate with international development teams and provide support when needed.
  • Act as a role model for junior team members by taking ownership of your work while keeping the team’s success in focus.

What you bring to Krones.digital

  • At least 5 years of experience in an advanced programming language (e.g., C#, C++), with strong expertise in modern development tools and frameworks.
  • Python experience is highly valued but not mandatory – we offer internal training, but willingness to work with Python is essential.
  • A passion for clean code, architecture, and writing tests as much as production code.
  • Proven experience (2+ years) in developing, deploying, and managing containers with Docker, plus familiarity with Kubernetes and a strong desire to master it.
  • Hands-on experience with DevOps tools (e.g., Git, Jenkins, CI/CD, Ansible, Terraform, Prometheus, Grafana, JFrog, Salt, Chef).
  • Knowledge of event-driven architecture and cloud-native environments.
  • Additional experience with AWS cloud platform, React, and SQL/NoSQL databases is a plus.
  • Ideally, experience in digital product development and agile methodologies.
